The Best Movies of All Time According to Me

My Top 3 Movies

The Cat in a Hat

This is such a classic movie. It has a great story with a fundamental lesson for kids as well as adult inuendos - perfect for all the family.

10 Things I Hate About You

Teen love at its best. Great casting - always a sight to the eye - and great soundtrack. This movie has everything.

Monte Carlo

Another teen love story, but this one is set in the beautiful city of Monte Carlo. The plot is a little bit predictable, but the scenery and the cast make up for it.
